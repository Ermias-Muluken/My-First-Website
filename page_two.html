Me and Chess

How did I start playing?

I remember playing chess in desktop computers when I was child like in lower grades. But honestly I do not remember how i played or even understood the game back then. So let me jump to the time I consciously started to play chess. It began when I was in High school. In my way to school there is a place that is kind of simple lounge that i pass through everyday and I was always attracted to the gathered people there. They were always there playing chess every single day. And I was curious that what made them to play so seriously but at the same time they chatter so much and laugh a lot.

Then I started to just stand and watch them play for some quiet days. One day one of them asked me to come and play with them I told him that i don’t know to play. Then he recommend some online videos to start of from the ground and know some basic things about chess( like movements ,checkmate and some opening). After Completing the recommended videos I asked to play with them. They asked the beginner player among them to play with me. We started to play and he smacked the heck out of me. I remember going home so angry and I couldn’t sleep thinking about the games. After this I watched some additional videos and try to understand the underlying concept of chess. I started playing with chess bots and got better. I returned to place and challenged him again. This time I also won a few games and that boost my confidence and started to play on forward

What about now?

Well now I am university student and far from my home town where I stared to play chess. Since there is no close place and much free time available to play chess. I switched to play online in sites like Chess.com and Lichess.org. I go in to this platforms as much as time I get to play with players all over the world. It is so cool to play with people that are from all over the world.
